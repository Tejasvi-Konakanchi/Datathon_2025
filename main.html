ModeCraft

Analysis report for ecommerce store with the aim of increasing revenue and profits.

2025 DubsTech Datathon Neil Bhalla, Geith Amum, Tejasvi Konakanchi, Nathan Dang, Trinity Kwon

ModeCraft have collected and processed over 500,000 orders for a diverse global clientele as a business and now want us to review their data and provide them with business recommendations. Our team has created multiple data-visualization dashboards for our client to understand their seasonal revenue at a glance.

Revenue Cycle

Question: Is there any seasonality in purchases for certain products?

Observation:

  1. ModeCraft has the highest revenue in winter at the beginning of the year.
  2. During this season there were huge spikes in revenue - peaking over 2 million and 1.7 million, respectively.
  3. Other months falter in comparison, hovering around 600K-800K.

Conclusion: Certain products show clear seasonality, raking up the revenue.

Business Strategy proposed:

  1. Launch major marketing campaign starting late December (sales promotions, gift cards).
  2. Stock up on top-performing products heavily (like the ones that dominated the charts this year).
  3. Offer limited-time bundles relevant to the top performing product to capitalize on holiday shopping enthusiasm
