ANALYSIS 02

Optimal Store Hours

Question:

What are the optimal store operating hours and days?

Observation:

  • Tuesdays and Thursdays generate the highest revenues, with customer transactions peaking between 10 AM and 1 PM
  • Stores should prioritize operating between 9 AM and 4 PM, with reduced hours after 5 PM
  • Sundays have the lowest sales and shouldn't be a major focus for marketing efforts.

Interpretation:

  • Customers are most active during mid-morning to early afternoon on weekdays
  • Sundays and late evenings are inefficient operating times

Conclusion:

Maximizing store operations between 9 AM to 4 PM on high-performing weekdays will drive efficiency and revenue.

Business Strategy Proposed:

  • Concentrate staffing and promotions on Tuesdays and Thursdays, especially 10AM - 1PM.
  • Reduce hours after 5PM and minimize Sunday operations unless targeted promotions are introduced.

ANALYSIS 03

Impact on Sales

Question:

How does Modecraft's geographic footprint impact sales?

Observation:

  • Strongest sales concentrations are in North America, Western Europe, Australia, and Brazil
  • Limited sales presence in Asia, Eastern Europe, and most of Africa

Interpretation:

  • Modecraft already has a foothold in several mature retail markets.
  • Emerging markets, particularly in Asia-Pacific, remain largely untapped.

Conclusion:

There is potential to both consolidate current strongholds and expand into new growth markets.

Business Strategy Proposed:

  • Reinforce marketing and customer loyalty programs in North America and Europe
  • Conduct market research and pilot campaigns in promoting regions like Southeast Asia
